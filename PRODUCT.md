# APO — Autonomous Patient Organization

## Register

Product

## Users

Patients who just received a diagnosis — anxious, scanning for clarity, not reading marketing copy. Hackathon judges watching a 90-second live demo where agent activity and charter alignment must read instantly.

## Product purpose

APO is an AI-native fiduciary that investigates treatment options, checks coverage, contacts providers, and returns a charter-aligned action plan. The UI proves agency (live agent feed, voice call) and fiduciary duty (explicit charter checks), not chatbot polish.

## Brand voice

Calm, clinical, direct. Names what each agent is doing. No hype, no reassurance theater.

## Personality

Trustworthy · Methodical · Patient-first

## Anti-references

- Purple gradients and glassmorphism
- Inter, generic SaaS dark mode, rainbow agent color coding
- Side-stripe feed borders and nested identical cards
- "Welcome to our platform" / "Boost your productivity" copy
- Gradient text, bouncing loaders, modal-first flows

## Design principles

1. **Charter check is the proof moment** — aligned/flagged states must dominate the plan panel visually.
2. **Activity feed shows work, not decoration** — agent entries are scannable log lines, not cards within cards.
3. **Restraint carries meaning** — color appears for status (running, aligned, flagged), not for brand flair.
4. **Readable under stress** — high contrast, generous line height, no uppercase label spam.

## Accessibility

WCAG AA contrast on all text. Status never conveyed by color alone — always paired with label or icon. Focus rings on all interactive elements.
